Prijedlog dizajna za website "Art is Online" / First design dor webside "Art is Online"

Dizajn websajta za jedan od projekata Youth Power organizacije, U nastavku možete vidjeti dizajn za dekstop uređaje. Projekat će biti objavljen po završetku - 10.10.2021 Design of website for one of the projects of Youth Power organisation, You can see design for desktop devices. This project is under the construction in Wordpress. This version of design will be responsive by the time the project release date: 10.10.2021.
